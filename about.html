Hello!

My name is Matthew and I am an aspiring GIS analyst. I first learned about GIS through my Environmental Systems - Earth Science B.S. program at UC San Diego. Since then, I've continued learning about mapping and cartography, as I am currently working towards a certificate degree in GIS at Mt. San Jacinto college. I love GIS because it blends the creativity and freedom of art with the rigor of science. I hope to combine my knowledge and passion for Earth science and the environment with map making, statistical analysis, computer science, and everything that makes GIS a promising career. Besides mapping, some other interests I have include making music, cooking, painting, making 3D art, and being outdoors. I care about conservation efforts and working to make the world a better, more sustainable place. As an avid learner, I try to include all of my interests in my maps. Currently, I am seeking an entry position or internship in the field. For any inquiry, don't hesitate to send me an email. Until next time, take care and keep mapping!
